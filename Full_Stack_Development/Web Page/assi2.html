Cymbopogon citratus

"Gavati Chaha" is the Marathi name for lemongrass, scientifically known as Cymbopogon citratus. It is a tropical plant with a distinct citrusy aroma, widely used in both culinary and medicinal contexts

Plumeria or Frangipani

The English name for the "chafa" flower is Plumeria or Frangipani. It's also commonly known as the Temple Tree or Nosegay. Plumeria is a genus of flowering plants known for their fragrant and vibrant flowers.

Holy Basil

The English name for Tulsi is Holy Basil. It is also sometimes referred to as Indian Basil or Sacred Basil.
